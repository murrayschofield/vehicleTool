Getting Started Guide

1. Click on "Enter Vehicle Inputs" on the side menu and enter vehicle inputs.

2. Click on "Define Drive Cycle" on the side menu and define the drive cycle, you can choose from standard cycles, or constant speeds and other scenarios.

3. Click on the "Run Simulation" Button to generate results. You have to do this every time you change an input value.

4. Under "Simulation Results" in the side menu you can explore many different results and graphs from the simulation.
